Our Work

There are a lot of tasks in medicine field which are repetitive and don’t necessarily require medical expertise. There is a need to optimize these processes and resources in-order to streamline the working of medical expects and take away their burden of non-medical tasks such as booking appointments, compiling medical history of patients, compiling laboratory results. This proposed work uses website-based user interface for booking appointments, laboratory tests and user interface for doctors to see medical history of patients and lab reports to design a system which reduces the burden of medical professionals from desk-work and let them focus on providing care and support to the patients. This serves as a very handy and feasible resource for doctors as well as patients as it provides ease of accessing and updating information.
